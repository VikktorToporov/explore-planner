Welcome to the Explore Planner

Your journey through the cosmos begins here. Plan your voyage with an intuitive, visually-driven interface.

Two Ways to Add Locations

Precision or exploration? Choose your method.

Select Place & Date

Use the dropdown to pick a place, then choose a date. Your destination is instantly added to your journey.

 Click the Globe

Spin the interactive 3D globe and simply click. The coordinates are captured and added as a new point.

Dynamic View Options

See the universe from multiple perspectives.

  • Seamlessly toggle between interactive Globe and classic Map views.
  • Explore different celestial bodies—Earth, Moon, Jupiter, and Mars - with a simple dropdown.

Effortless Import & Export

Your data, your way. Take it with you.

  • Import a list of pre-planned locations from a CSV file.
  • Download your current itinerary as a CSV or a beautifully formatted PDF document.

Ready to Start Exploring?

Use the form or globe, import your data, and export and share it to the world.
